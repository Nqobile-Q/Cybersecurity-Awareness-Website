Why Cybersecurity Matters

Cybersecurity matters because it protects personal data, prevents financial loss, keeps systems running, and defends against hackers. Without it, people, businesses, and even governments are vulnerable to attacks. cybersecurity keeps us safe in the digital world.

Every day, millions of people fall victim to phishing, hacking, and online scams. Awareness is your first defense.
